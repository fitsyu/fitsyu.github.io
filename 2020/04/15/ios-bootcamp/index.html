The Gojek iOS Bootcamp

Is a special learning program held by Gojek for its internal employee or known as Go-Troops.

This program magically transformed previously non-iOS developers into full fledged iOS developers who can contribute to the Gojek iOS codebase within less than two weeks.

The program is rigorous, fast-paced and fun. The coach mentor from their own wealth technical knowledge and experiences. Mentee enjoy the learning experience, share hardship together and support each other.
We started off from the basics of Swift as we learn at the same time how to use the tools. Then slowly moved to iOS specific development activities and implemented the common development best practices.

Finally we were challenged to create a Go-Food clone. Probably because most of the mentors are from Go-Food team.

I was teaming up with Jo. We split the work by the type of layer. Jo chose to work on UI layer, so I took the data layer.
And the rest of layer done in collaboration. Jo is more to data science and electrical engineering but he has potential to be a great iOS developer too.

If you want to almost instantly become an iOS developer, then Gojek iOS Bootcamp, is definitely a correct choice for you.

I remembered that the coach said on the first day that to develop an iOS app is to build something beatifully and indeed throughout this program we have come to beautiful learning experience.
